IMLS-funded curriculum development project producing modular open science lessons for library professionals. Lessons cover open access, data management plans, research data repositories, open source sustainability, and reproducible research workflows.

Designed for "train the trainers" delivery: librarians learn the material and pedagogy, then deliver to their local research communities.

Governance pathway through the Library Carpentry Advisory Group for long-term maintenance.
