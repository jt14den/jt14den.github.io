Co-PI (UCLA lead) on a $1.85M Sloan Foundation award establishing Open Source Program Offices across six University of California campuses. The grant funds infrastructure for research software sustainability including licensing support, governance structures, industry partnerships, and education pipelines.

UCLA role centers on the Education Working Group, developing modular training resources for researchers and librarians.
